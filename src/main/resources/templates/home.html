GOLD's GYM


Gold's Gym is a globally renowned fitness brand that has made its mark in India. With a strong legacy dating back to 1965 in Venice Beach, California, Gold's Gym has become synonymous with fitness excellence and innovation. Gold’s Gym India carries the legacy ahead in the home country since its inception in 2002. World-class fitness facilities and a comprehensive range of workout programs tailored to meet the needs of diverse fitness enthusiasts is what sets us apart from others. Gold's Gym India, a part of this esteemed legacy, has expanded its presence across various cities, aiming to empower individuals to achieve their fitness goals, regardless of their fitness levels or aspirations. It's a fitness haven that combines state-of-the-art equipment, expert trainers, and a supportive community to foster a holistic approach to wellness.

Basic Plan

  • Price: ₹1000 per month
  • Access to basic gym equipment such as treadmills, stationary bikes, and weight machines.
  • No access to classes or personal training sessions. Limited hours of operation, typically during off-peak times.

Standard Plan

  • Price: ₹1500 per month
  • Access to a wider range of equipment including cardio machines, free weights, and strength training machines.
  • Limited access to group fitness classes like yoga, spinning, and aerobics.
  • Access to basic amenities such as showers and locker rooms.
  • Extended hours of operation including evenings and weekends.

Premium Plan

  • Price: ₹2000 per month
  • Full access to all gym facilities including equipment, classes, and amenities.
  • Unlimited access to group fitness classes led by certified instructors.
  • Option for personal training sessions with experienced trainers.
  • Access to additional amenities such as sauna, steam room, and towel service.
  • Priority access during peak hours and busy periods.
